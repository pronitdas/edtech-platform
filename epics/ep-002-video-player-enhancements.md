# [Epic] Video Player and Content Integration Enhancements

## Background
Based on issue #3-update-frontend-video-player.md, we need to enhance our video player to support chapter navigation and improve content integration.

## Technical Details

### Components to Update
- `VideoPlayer`: Add support for chapter markers and navigation
- `MainCourse`: Improve integration between video and other content types
- `ContentToggle`: Enhance synchronization between video and notes

### Tasks
1. Add chapter timeline markers to video player
2. Implement active chapter highlighting during playback
3. Create improved content synchronization between video and notes
4. Add hover tooltips for timeline markers

## Acceptance Criteria
- Users can navigate to specific chapters by clicking markers or sidebar items
- Currently playing chapter is visually highlighted
- Timeline shows markers for all chapters with proper styling for content types
- Video position and content display are properly synchronized
